Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Halo does not collect any user data.

We believe in complete privacy. All processing happens on your device.

Data Collection

Halo itself collects zero personal information, usage data, or analytics.

  • No tracking
  • No analytics
  • No data collection
  • No remote servers

Third-Party Services

When you use certain tools within Halo, they may interact with third-party services:

  • Weather: Weather data providers may have their own privacy policies
  • Web Search: Search providers handle queries according to their policies
  • Other Tools: Any external service accessed through tools operates under its own privacy policy

Halo does not store or process data from these services beyond displaying results to you.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud sync:

  • Your data syncs across your devices using Apple's CloudKit
  • This is entirely optional
  • Sync is handled by Apple's secure infrastructure
  • See Apple's privacy policy for iCloud services

On-Device Processing

All AI processing and data storage happens locally on your device. Your conversations, files, and personal information never leave your device unless you explicitly share them through iOS sharing features.

Contact

If you have any questions about privacy, please visit our support page.
